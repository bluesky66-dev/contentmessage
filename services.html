Our Services

Serviced apartments are a fantastic alternative to a hotel when staying away from home, especially for an extended period of time. Being able to enjoy an authentic home away from home experience, is a key benefit of staying with Homezie.

SERVICED

A serviced apartment is approximately five times the size of a standard luxury hotel room and offers you the ability to live in a whole apartment, with more privacy and independence, whilst still enjoying the benefits of hotel-style housekeeping and concierge service.

Your apartment will be cleaned on a weekly basis and your linen and towels replaced, just like in a hotel. Unlike a hotel, WiFi is free, and every serviced apartment contains a fully equipped kitchen, which means you can cook or do laundry whenever you like, without paying a penny extra!

UNSERVICED

Our short-term rental apartments are the ideal solution for those cost conscious clients who need a spacious, clean and cost effective “home from home”. You get all of the benefits of renting a whole beautifully furnished apartment, but with all of the utilities and taxes included as standard in one easy to understand rate. There are no hidden fees to pay, no references to complete and you can stay for as long as you like.

Relocation

Whether a family or single assignee, deciding on a temporary home in an unfamiliar host city is not always straightforward and can often be a stressful time for those involved. It’s a time when people need reassurance and the added peace of mind, knowing every element of their move is being managed effectively. Serviced apartments provide a flexible solution for short-term accommodation needs and a host of lifestyle benefits for individuals and families relocating. With added comfort and reassurance, the transition can be much smother and beneficial for both employer and employee with greater all round focus and productivity.
